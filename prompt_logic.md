# Prompt Logic Documentation

## Goal

Generate website-ready content for a local beauty salon.

---

## Prompt Version 1

Act as an expert website copywriter.

Create homepage content for a beauty salon.

Include:
- Headline
- Subheadline
- Benefits
- Call To Action

Tone:
Friendly, modern and professional.

Target Audience:
Women aged 18-50 looking for beauty and wellness services.

---

## Result

The output was professional but lacked emotional appeal.

---

## Prompt Version 2

Act as a conversion-focused website copywriter.

Create homepage content for a beauty salon called "Glow & Grace Beauty Salon".

Requirements:
- Emotional headline
- Trust-building language
- Customer-focused benefits
- Strong booking CTA

Tone:
Warm, premium and welcoming.

Target Audience:
Women seeking beauty treatments, self-care and confidence boosting services.

---

## Improvements

- Better customer engagement
- More persuasive language
- Stronger CTA
- Improved readability

---

## Final Prompt

Create complete website copy for a beauty salon including homepage, services page and CTA section.

Business Name:
Glow & Grace Beauty Salon

Services:
- Hair Styling
- Facials
- Bridal Makeup
- Nail Art
- Spa Treatments

Tone:
Friendly, luxurious and trustworthy.

Goal:
Increase appointment bookings.

Output:
Website-ready copy.
